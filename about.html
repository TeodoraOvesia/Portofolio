What I love?

I love to travel! It gives me a lot of energy and helps me reset my mind. Discovering new places is very exciting and it is a very good technique to get a boost of happiness. A summer holiday, a ski one or simply a city break for the weekend, it doesn’t matter. I only need to have something planned and I am happy.

What I enjoy?

I really enjoy cooking and then sitting comfortable on the couch under a blanket and eating what I cooked, while watching TV. I would cook and eat pasta every day, but also cakes, brownies or cupcakes. I also took some cooking lessons so I can cook even more for me… and for friends and family. I don’t enjoy only cooking, but also eating. That’s why every time I am on holiday I try to eat the traditional food from that area and try lots of new things (if they contain pasta is even better).

What I do?

In my spare time I like to walk around the city and reconnect with the nature. It always helps me to recharge my batteries. I like spending time with friends and family, playing with every dog I see and sleep.
